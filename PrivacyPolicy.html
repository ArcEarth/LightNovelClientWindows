Personal Information, credential, and statistic information Usage

All the credential you provide inside application WILL ONLY be used inside the application to signin to lknovel.lightnovel.cn, an encrpted session key string produced by this login process will be used to retrieve your "Favorite Collection" and request to comment on your behave.

The login session will usually have the life time about one month depend on server side detail.

All the personal ans statistic information, which include the credential you entered and the history data you generate while using the application, WILL NOT be collected by the client developer.

In general, the application and service is not concern about any sensitive personal information or real person identity. For details about the services side policy, please regard to the User-Terms in sign-up process.

Personal Information and credential storage

Credential(UserID and password) you provide is stored locally and encrypted inside your Windows machine(Tablet/Phone) by Microsoft standard credential storage.

Due to the fact that the sensitivity of your usage data is trival, the application stored your usage data (history and favortite collection) with un-encrypted manner and roams across your devices for synchronizing purpose.
